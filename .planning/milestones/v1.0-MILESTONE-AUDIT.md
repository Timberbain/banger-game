The Phase 3 verification flagged 2 gaps:

**Both gaps are CLOSED** — not blockers for milestone.

### Moved to Out of Scope (4 requirements)

| Requirement | Decision | Rationale |
|-------------|----------|-----------|
| ACCT-01: Account creation (username/password) | Out of Scope | Game is fully playable without accounts; not needed for MVP |
| ACCT-02: Session persists across refresh | Out of Scope | Reconnection tokens provide sufficient session handling |
| ACCT-03: Stats tracked (wins, losses, per character) | Out of Scope | Per-match stats exist; persistent tracking deferred |
| ACCT-04: View own stats | Out of Scope | Deferred — no persistent storage in v1.0 |

**Decision:** User moved ACCT-01-04 to Out of Scope. The game is fully playable without a persistent account system. Reconnection tokens in sessionStorage provide sufficient session management for v1.0.
